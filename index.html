"Shinbyeong" Official Soundtrack

May 8, 2019

Check out the official book soundtrack to "Shinbyeong" on our Bandcamp page.

Our good friend Rou Hun Fan asked us to create music for the first two chapters of his book. You can buy the book from Amazon.

The music for the first chapter is a dark and chaotic techno track called "Army Of Lost Souls". After recording our trusty Volcas we added some ambient textures using the Spacecraft granular synth for iOS.

For the second track, "Neon Wedding: Cyber Sunset", we explored a previously unfamiliar genre: vaporwave. We recorded a 'retro' beat and slowed it down quite dramatically to get to that wavy sound. Rou Hun asked his Korean friend EJay to record herself while reading parts of the second chapter, translated to Korean. We then added some effects and recorded the mix on cassette tape to get that vintage sound.

Our First EP: "M"

August 19, 2018

We've released our first EP, called "M", which you can download from Bandcamp.

"M" contains four tracks extracted from three different jams from the first half of 2018. All tracks are played on four Korg Volcas, hooked up to several effect pedals and controllers.

Our friend Levi made the cover artwork. You can buy a limited edition print from his webshop, along with a Bandcamp code for a free download!
